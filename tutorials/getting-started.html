Welcome to Lander Game!

Your goal is to safely land the spacecraft on the ground without crashing.

How to Play

  1. Select a level from the menu
  2. Build your control code using Blockly blocks
  3. Click "Run Code" to start the simulation
  4. Watch your lander and see if it lands safely!

Safe Landing

To land safely, your total impact velocity must be 10 m/s or less. Land too fast and your spacecraft will explode!

Manual Control

Press M during simulation to toggle manual control.

  • W/S - Increase/decrease throttle
  • A/D - Rotate left/right

Your code with supercede whatever manual inputs you use.
